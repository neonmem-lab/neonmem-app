## What counts as commercial use?

Broadly: using Neonmem in or for a business, a for-profit product, paid
consulting, or any activity with an anticipated commercial application —
including internal use inside a for-profit company. If you are unsure whether
your use is covered by the free noncommercial license, assume it is commercial
and reach out.

## Getting a commercial license

A commercial license removes the noncommercial restriction and can include
priority support and updates. Pricing depends on company size and usage.

- **Contact:** sales@neonmem.com
- Please include: your company, intended use, and approximate number of seats.

## Why this model

Neonmem is free for individuals and nonprofits forever. Commercial licensing
funds continued development. This is a dual-licensing arrangement: the copyright
holder offers the software under the noncommercial license to the public and,
separately, under a paid commercial license to businesses.
